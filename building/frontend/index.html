What is Vastu Shastra?

Vastu Shastra is an ancient Indian science of architecture and construction that creates harmony between your home and natural elements. Our expert consultants help you design spaces that promote health, wealth, and happiness.

Benefits of Vastu-Compliant Homes:

  • Enhanced positive energy flow
  • Improved health and well-being
  • Better financial prospects
  • Harmonious family relationships
  • Peaceful living environment

Essential Vastu Guidelines

Main Entrance

North, East, or Northeast facing entrance brings prosperity and positive energy

Master Bedroom

Southwest direction ensures stability and harmonious relationships

Kitchen

Southeast direction with cooking facing East promotes health

Living Room

North or East direction creates welcoming family space

Water Elements

Northeast corner for water attracts prosperity

Puja Room

Northeast or East direction enhances spiritual energy

Vastu Compliance Checklist

Do's

  • Keep Northeast corner light and clutter-free
  • Ensure main door opens clockwise
  • Place heavy furniture in Southwest
  • Keep center of house (Brahmasthan) open
  • Use bright colors in North and East
  • Install windows in North and East walls

Don'ts

  • Avoid toilets in Northeast corner
  • Don't place mirrors in bedroom facing bed
  • Avoid stairs in center of house
  • Don't build kitchen in Southwest
  • Avoid dark colors in North and East
  • Don't keep broken or unused items

4

Ensure good ventilation and natural light in all rooms
